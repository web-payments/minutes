The PaySwarm Community discusses and develops systems to enable legal, copyright-aware, peer-to-peer digital media distribution.

The PaySwarm Community

You can affect the future of PaySwarm by collaborating with the open community that is involved with designing and implementing this technology. There are no qualifications necessary to join the group - if you're interested in digital content distribution, peer-to-peer development, media, digital culture or the Web, join us. There are a few ways that you can keep up with what is going on as well as take part in designing the future of digital content distribution on the web:

  • PaySwarm Development - The mailing list where all design and implementation discussion happens.
  • @payswarm on Twitter - A twitter stream that summarizes the latest PaySwarm news.
  • #payswarm on IRC - Real-time PaySwarm chat on FreeNode. You can use a web browser to connect.
  • Audio Records of Meetings - We keep a complete audio log of all PaySwarm meetings available to anyone that is interested.
